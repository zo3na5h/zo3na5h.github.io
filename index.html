About Us

Hi, I'm Pete Nash! I'm a Level 2 qualified Gym Instructor and a Level 3 qualified Personal Trainer with a Level 2 qualification in Kettlebells based in the Worsley area of Manchester. I've always loved sports and exercise, and a few years behind a desk working a 9-5 finally convinced me to follow my dream of qualifying to be a personal trainer.

I'm passionate about fitness and want to find a way to get everyone involved. I understand that training isn't a one-size-fits-all kind of thing, and that's why I'm excited to work with you to see what suits you, your lifestyle, and your goals.

I'm an advocate of exercise being fun and enjoyable - it's the only way exercising stays sustainable. Invest in yourself and let's work together to create what works for you so you can feel fitter, stronger, faster!

Mission

Our mission first and foremost is: Fitness For All.

Nobody should feel left out when it comes to exercise. It's very much a for-everyone thing. That's why here at Pete Nash Personal Training we like to offer a non-judgemental service to everyone no matter where you are in your journey or what your abilities are. All we ask is that you are wanting to make the change yourself, because that's the most important thing!

We love to encourage and support. We aren't your drill sergeants, we aren't here to force you to do things that you hate doing (*cough* burpees). We just want to see you get the best out of your body.

If this sounds like the sort of personal training you're looking for and you're ready to take your training to the next level, then please get in contact with us!
